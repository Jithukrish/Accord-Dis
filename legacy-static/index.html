Our Story

From a single brewery to a nationwide name

Accord Distillers & Brewers Pvt. Ltd. — part of the Accord Group — entered the world of brewing with a simple ambition: to make bold, honest, super strong beer. From the first cases in 2013 to over a million cases a month today, the spirit hasn't changed — quality, strength and pride in every bottle.

  1. 2009

    Accord enters the world of beer manufacturing.

  2. 2011

    The brewing unit is set up and readied for production.

  3. 2013

    Commercial production begins — the first Accord beers reach the market.

  4. Today

    Over a million cases a month and four flagship strong beers.
