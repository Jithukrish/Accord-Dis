1. BUSINESS OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------
Business Name:   La Revoltosa
Tagline/Slogan:  "La burbuja Ibérica" (The Iberian Bubble)
Brand voice:     "Muuuucho gas" (lots of fizz) — bold, witty,
                 unfiltered, social.
Category:        Spanish carbonated soft drink / beverage brand
Heritage:        70+ years of history
Parent Company:  Refrescos del Atlántico
                 (https://refrescosdelatlantico.com) — a family
                 company with over 100 years of history.
Design / Branding Agency: waka (https://somoswaka.com)

Positioning:
La Revoltosa is a heritage soda brand built for social
occasions — bar gatherings, snacks (picoteo), and toasts with
good company — rather than everyday at-home consumption. It
markets itself as "tu excusa perfecta para quedarte un ratito
más" (your perfect excuse to stay a little longer).

--------------------------------------------------------------
2. BRAND STORY / HISTORY (Mi Vida / Historia)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Founded:   1953, in Madrid.
Founders:  Three beverage distributors — Manuel, Félix and
           Bonifacio — who joined forces to compete collectively
           against larger rivals.

Origin of the name:
Named after Mari-Pepa "La Revoltosa," the protagonist of a
Spanish zarzuela (operetta). The character embodied rebellion
and outspokenness — "La que no se callaba. La que armaba jaleo"
(the one who wouldn't stay silent, the one who caused a stir).

Key Milestones:
- 1953: Brand founded in Madrid by Manuel, Félix and Bonifacio.
- 1955: Joins UNFAES, a cooperative of manufacturers, expanding
        beyond the three original founders.
- 1964: UNFAES becomes BECAESA, launching a franchise model that
        expands distribution across Spain.
- 1970s: Peak brand visibility — strong presence on radio, TV
        and in bars; becomes a nationwide manufacturer.
- 1998: Acquired by Refrescos del Atlántico, a family company
        with 100+ years of history.

Brand philosophy:
Despite many redesigns over the decades, the brand insists
"yo siempre he sido la misma: La Revoltosa de siempre" (I've
always been the same: the eternal Revoltosa) — consistency in
spirit despite visual evolution.

3. PRODUCTS (Mis Bebidas / Productos)
--------------------------------------------------------------
All products are carbonated beverages sold in bottles.

1. Formidable Gaseosa — Sparkling soft drink
   Tagline: "Better alone than in bad company"
   A casual fizzy drink for social moments without elaborate plans.

2. Sifón — Plain sparkling water (soda siphon)
   Tagline: "Point and shoot"
   Clear, simple carbonated water — a "blessed invention."

3. Limón — Lemon-flavored sparkling drink
   Tagline: "A prick on the tongue"
   Acidic, honest and witty; for those who like tart flavors.

4. Naranja — Orange-flavored sparkling drink
   Tagline: "Contagion of good vibes"
   Energetic and joyful; unites people; for all ages.

5. Lima Limón — Lime + lemon blend
   Tagline: "Neither without the other"
   Refreshing citrus with character and subtle sweetness.

6. Cola — Cola-flavored drink
   Tagline: "Where I go, things get good"
   A celebratory drink that shines in social settings.

7. Cola Zero — Sugar-free cola
   Tagline: "Zero sugar. Zero drama."
   Same taste and personality, without added sugar.

8. Cola Zero Zero — Sugar-free & caffeine-free cola (kid-friendly)
   Tagline: "Sweet dreams, little rebel"
   Made for children; flavor without stimulants.

9. Tónica — Tonic water
   Tagline: "Bitter and witty"
   Sophisticated, bitter-origin drink for refined occasions.

4. COMMUNITY (Mi Gente / Revoltosos)
--------------------------------------------------------------
The "Mi Gente" section celebrates the brand's community — the
"revoltosos." Core idea: "tu excusa perfecta para quedarte un
ratito más" (your perfect excuse to stay a little longer),
for when you have free time and want to enjoy it in good company.

Social invitation: "Sígueme, que no muerdo" (Follow me, I don't
bite) — daily content, collaborations and brand updates on
Instagram.

Implied community values:
- Valuing simple moments over elaborate plans
- Seeking companionship and shared experiences
- Casual, social gatherings
- Appreciation for an authentic, unfiltered brand personality

The site offers two engagement paths: general contact and
collaboration opportunities.
